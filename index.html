Creative Design

At Hapka Landscaping, we carefully consider your vision and needs to provide a creative new look for your home. Our goal is to develop a landscape design for your new or existing home, and to add the enjoyment of getting up in the morning or coming home at night to your newly landscaped home. We will work diligently with your concerns and desires.

Landscape Renovation

We can help you with your new or existing home, from plantings, retaining walls, patios (brick or concrete with brick inlay) to walkways, ponds to complete interrogated water features, fire pits to custom grilling areas or a combination of all the above. We can also repair your grade or lawn after you've had a remodeling project done.

Past Projects

We have worked with people in all corners of the greater Milwaukee area to provide satisfying results on everything from lawn maintenance to full reconstruction and renovation.
